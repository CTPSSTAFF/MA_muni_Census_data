The goal of this project is to produce a table that displays, for each municipality in MA, the following statistics:

  • population (P1_001N)
  • households
  • families
  • population density
  • housing units (H1_001N or H001001, occupied units = H002003 or H1_002N)
  • housing unit density
  • % population by race (one race = P1_002N thru _008N, two+ = P1_009N)
  • % Hispanic/Latino population (acs5, B03001_003 - _031)
  • average household size ()
  • average family size
  • % of households w/ children under 18
  • % of households w/ married couples
  • % of households w/ female householder w/o husband
  • % of households that are not families
  • % of households that are only individuals
  • % of households that are only an individual age 65+ living alone
  • % population by age group (<18, 18-24, 25-44, 45-64, 65+)
  • median age
  • males per 100 females
  • 18+ males per 100 18+ females
  • median income (for households and families)
  • male vs. female median income
  • income per capita
  • % of population living below poverty line (plus % of <18 and 65+ population)
  • % of families living below poverty line
